The Start

When you join the server, you will see a hall; this is the rules' hall. Read the rules carefully! The rules are translated in multiple languages, such as Italian, English, Spanish, and even some Asian languages; Choose your native language. By entering the spawn, you agree on what you just read, and everything you do from now on, on the server, you are the responsible.
